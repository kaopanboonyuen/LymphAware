This structured bias disruption yields acquisition-invariant yet morphology-preserving embeddings, improving robustness in heterogeneous multi-source settings without requiring verified institutional labels.

LymphAware reframes domain generalization as representation-level shortcut decomposition, advancing reliable and clinically meaningful lymphoma diagnosis.
